About Planning Assistant

An AI-assisted workspace that connects Jira Align planning intent with Jira delivery data so product area leads do not have to manually stitch together exports, inbox context, capacity spreadsheets, and expert judgement.

Jira and Jira Align connected flow

  • Fetch carryover stories and epics from Jira/Jira Align so existing work is treated as UPDATE, not accidental new scope.
  • Use Jira velocity and team history as the starting point for capacity, then adjust for holidays, L&D, PTO, and other unavailable time.
  • Keep Jira Align allocation intent visible through sizing, team fit, prioritization, and final Jira publishing.
  • Prepare a final publish list where every row is clearly a Jira CREATE or UPDATE decision.

Where AI bridges the manual gaps

  • Turns intake notes, transcripts, and links into suggested epics, stories, and acceptance criteria.
  • Suggests story points using scope, acceptance criteria, dependency, and historical delivery signals.
  • Suggests low-level design considerations that help teams decide low-level sizing with less back-and-forth.
  • Suggests priority based on story relationships, dependencies, blockers, risk, and business value.
  • Highlights allocation and capacity gaps that normally require a senior planning expert to detect manually.
